Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our packages

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept mobile money (MTN, Airtel), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my package later?
Yes, you can change your package at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
How does the referral commission work?
When you refer another business to OBlinks, you earn a percentage of their subscription fee based on your current package. This commission is paid monthly and can be withdrawn to your mobile money account or bank.
What does "pinning" mean?
Pinning means your business card will be featured at the top of relevant search results and category pages for the duration specified in your package. This increases visibility and engagement with potential customers.
Is there a contract or long-term commitment?
No, all our packages are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel at any time, though we'd be sorry to see you go!
