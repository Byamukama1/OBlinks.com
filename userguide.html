Sales Agent Guide

A sales agent is a professional who represents a company and sells its products or services, often working independently and earning commission-based income. As an OBlinks Sales Agent, you earn commissions by selling our packages to business owners through referring their businesses to our platform. This guide explains how to maximize your earnings.

1

Get Your Referral Link

Log in to your OBlinks account and navigate to the "Earnings Dashboard" to find your unique referral link.

2

Promote OBlinks

Share your referral link with business owners through WhatsApp, social media, or in-person meetings.

3

Assist with Signup

Help businesses sign up using your link and choose the right package for their needs.

4

Earn Commission

When a business you refer upgrades to a paid package, you earn commission based on your agent level.

Commission Structure

Your commission rate depends on your sales agent level:

  • Starter Agent: 20% commission on all referred packages
  • Professional Agent: 60% commission on all referred packages
  • Enterprise Agent: 80% commission on all referred packages

Commission is paid annually to your mobile money or bank account. You can track your earnings in the Earnings Dashboard.

Pro Tips for Sales Agents

  • Focus on businesses that would benefit most from online visibility
  • Use the "Add Business" button to help businesses sign up directly
  • Offer to help with the initial setup for businesses new to technology
  • Follow up with businesses that signed up but haven't upgraded
  • Leverage the Business Link feature to showcase success stories

Be Both a Business Owner AND Sales Agent

Many of our most successful users wear both hats! You can run your own business on OBlinks while earning commissions by referring other businesses.

Your dashboard makes it easy to switch between managing your business and tracking your referrals.

Business Owner Guide

List your business on OBlinks to reach more customers and grow your sales. This guide explains everything you need to know.

1

Choose a Package

Select a business package that fits your needs. We have free and paid options with different features.

2

Enter Business Details

Fill in your business name, description, location, category, and WhatsApp number.

3

Add Items & Services

List your products or services with prices and photos. Show customers exactly what you offer.

4

Submit & Pay

For paid packages, complete payment to activate your listing. Free listings go live immediately.

OBlinks Buttons & Features Guide

Here's what every button and feature does on OBlinks:

Add Business Button

Opens the form to list your business details, products, and services.

Where you see it:

Bottom navigation bar (center button)

Pay Now Button

Appears when you choose a paid package. Takes you to payment options.

Where you see it:

Business form footer for paid packages

View All Button

Shows all products/services from a business in one place.

Where you see it:

On each business card

Order Button

Opens an order form for customers to place orders directly.

Where you see it:

On each business card and product

Chat on WhatsApp

Starts a WhatsApp chat with the business directly.

Where you see it:

On each business card and product

Business Link Button

Opens your website, Facebook page, or other link you added.

Where you see it:

On your business card

Business Owner Tips

  • Use the pinning feature to keep your business visible
  • Consider upgrading your package for better visibility
  • Share your OBlinks listing on your social media
