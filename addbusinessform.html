Frequently Asked Questions

What does "pinning" mean?
Pinning features your business at the top of relevant search results and category pages for the specified duration.
How does the referral commission work?
You earn a percentage of referred businesses' subscription fees based on your current package. Paid annually to your mobile money or bank.
Can I change plans later?
Yes - upgrades apply immediately (prorated charge), downgrades take effect at next billing cycle.
