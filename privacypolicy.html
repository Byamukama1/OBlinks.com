Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Online Market UG ("we," "us," or "our"). We operate an online marketplace platform (website and mobile app) that connects business owners with customers in Uganda. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

By accessing or using Online Market UG, you consent to the practices described in this policy. We comply with Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 and adhere to GDPR principles for international users.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number when registering
  • Business Details: Business name, description, location, items, prices, and photos
  • Payment Data: Transaction details processed through Silicon Pay (we don't store card numbers)
  • Communications: Messages sent through our platform

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device model, operating system
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (with consent)

Cookies and Tracking:

  • Essential cookies for platform functionality
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) to understand user behavior
  • Advertising cookies to show relevant business listings

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process payments for advertising packages
  • Display business listings to potential customers
  • Enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • Improve and personalize our services
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Prevent fraud and ensure platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations in Uganda

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your information based on:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide our marketplace services
  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential cookies
  • Legitimate interests: Platform security, service improvements, and fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance: To meet regulatory requirements

5. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted partners to provide our services:

  • Silicon Pay: Payment processing (view their privacy policy at siliconpay.co.ug/privacy)
  • WhatsApp: For direct buyer-seller communications (messages occur outside our platform)
  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics (data is anonymized)
  • Cloud Service Providers: Secure data storage in encrypted servers
We never sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Until deletion request
  • Transaction records: 7 years for tax compliance
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 2 years

Security Measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit (SSL) and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant partners

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and personal data
  • Object to processing of your information
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Port your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information is primarily processed in Uganda. For international transfers (e.g., to EEA-based services), we use GDPR-compliant mechanisms like Standard Contractual Clauses to ensure adequate protection.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect children's data. If we become aware of such collection, we will promptly delete it.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through our platform or email. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.
