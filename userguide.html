Your Complete Guide to OBlinks

Welcome to OBlinks - Uganda's easiest way to list your business online. Whether you're selling clothes, food, or services, OBlinks helps you reach more customers and grow your sales. This guide explains everything you need to know.

How to Add Your Business

Follow these simple steps to list your business on OBlinks and start reaching customers:

1

Choose a Package

Select a business package that fits your needs. We have free and paid options with different features.

2

Enter Business Details

Fill in your business name, description, location, category, and WhatsApp number so customers can contact you.

3

Add Items & Services

List your products or services with prices and photos. Show customers exactly what you offer.

4

Submit & Pay (if needed)

For paid packages, complete payment to activate your listing. Free listings go live immediately.

Earn with Referrals

OBlinks lets you earn real money by helping other businesses join our platform. You earn commission only when businesses you refer upgrade to paid packages.

1

Share Your Link

Find your unique referral link in your Earnings Dashboard.

2

Business Joins

When a business signs up using your link, they become your referral.

3

Business Upgrades

When your referral upgrades to a paid package, you earn commission.

4

You Get Paid

Commission is paid annually to your mobile money or bank account.

How Commission Works

Your commission rate depends on your current package. For example:

  • Starter: 20% commission
  • Professional: 60% commission
  • Enterprise: 80% commission

You earn a percentage of your referral's package payment. Payments are made annually through Mobile Money or bank transfer.
